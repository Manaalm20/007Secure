Importance of Cybersecurity

Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting digital devices, networks, and sensitive information from unauthorized access, cyber attacks, and other cyber threats. It is crucial in preventing financial loss, reputation damage, physical harm, and ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of digital assets, maintaining the trust of customers, partners, and stakeholders.
